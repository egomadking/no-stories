about this site

Recipe stories can be tedious.

Sometimes the only thing is needed is a recipe. This site is an uncluttered reference for those who need a to see a recipe while cooking. Even without the atmosperics of a lenthy tale, I guarantee that there is more than one recipe that has ruined a restaraunt experience for a friend or family member.

About me
